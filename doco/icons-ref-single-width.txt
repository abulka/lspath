## **Tips for Avoiding Glitches:**
1. **Stick to ASCII** for maximum compatibility
2. **Test** unusual symbols in your target environment first
3. **Avoid** characters above U+FFFF (which are often problematic)
4. **Common safe blocks:** U+0020 to U+00FF (Latin-1 Supplement) and selected characters up to U+2600
